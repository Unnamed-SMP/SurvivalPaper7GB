Rules and regulations

 

Section A

1.       These rules may be updated at any time without notification from members of staff

2.       These rules must be followed. Please see Section D if you seek to find out what happens if you don’t.

3.       Staff members will and should enforce such rules and may enforce other regulations that they may deduce to be necessary

4.       The notation at the end of each rules dictates possible punishment

5.       These rules are expected to be read by all new members

6.       If you are caught breaking a given rule, you are expected to admit your fault. Failure to do this will increase punishment severity

7.       Every time you break a rule, severity increases.
